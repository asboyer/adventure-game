You follow the dwarf through the doors of the castle. As you step through the entrance, you step on a stone that triggers a booby trap. 

An arrow whistles through the air aimed right for your head. You barely dodge the arrow and fall to the ground.

The dwarf laughs. "You passed the first test. Follow me, your training begins now. You will become the warrior this realm needs."

You follow the dwarf down the dark corridor and brace yourself for the months of painful training that await you...
